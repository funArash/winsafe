Prefer importing this trait through the prelude:

use winsafe::prelude::*;

fn size_px(&self, hdc: Option<&HDC>) -> HrResult<SIZE>

Calls IPicture::get_Width and IPicture::get_Height, then converts the HIMETRIC units to pixels.

If hdc is not provided, the screen DC, retrieved with HWND::NULL.GetDC(), will be used.
